Creating VLANs on Single & Multiple Switches
packet tracer lab

Configured VLANs on one and then two switches in Cisco Packet Tracer. Assigned IP addresses to hosts, moved switch ports into different VLANs, and used 802.1Q trunking between switches. Verified VLAN separation by pinging hosts in the same and different VLANs to show how VLANs isolate traffic into separate broadcast domains.

Cisco Packet Tracer VLANs 802.1Q Trunking Broadcast Domains
